Lasagna

Description

John Chandler lasagna recipe is loved by everybody due to its incredibly customizable, so you can easily alter the ingredient list to suit your needs.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and 2 cloves of garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Two tablespoons of white sugar
  • Fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • An egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
    4. Layer the lasagna
    • Meat sauce
    • Noodles
    • Ricotta mixture
    • Mozzarella slices
    • Meat sauce
    • Parmesan cheese
    • Repeat the layers, then top with the remaining Parmesan
  5. In an oven preheated to 375 degrees F. Cover it with foil for the first 25 minutes, then let it cook uncovered for the final 25 minutes
  6. Let the lasagna rest for about 15 minutes before serving
